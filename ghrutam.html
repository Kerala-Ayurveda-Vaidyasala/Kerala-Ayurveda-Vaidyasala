Brahmi Ghritam

Brahmi Ghritam is a classical Ayurvedic formulation belonging to the category of Ghrita (medicated ghee). It is one of the most important Medhya Rasayana (nootropic rejuvenators) used for enhancing brain function, memory, and nervous system health. Prepared using Go Ghrita (cow’s ghee) as the base, this formulation is uniquely capable of delivering active herbal constituents across the blood–brain barrier, ensuring deep nourishment of the central nervous system. Brahmi Ghritam is especially valued for balancing Vata and Pitta in the mind, making it effective in conditions ranging from stress and memory issues to complex neurological disorders.

Common Traditional Uses

  • Medhya (enhances intellect and cognition) • Smritivardhaka (improves memory and recall) • Rasayana (rejuvenates nervous tissue) • Manas shamak (calms the mind) • Nidrajanana (promotes healthy sleep) • Vata–Pitta shamana (balances mental doshas) • Ojovardhaka (enhances vitality and resilience) • Srotoshodhana (clears channels of the brain and nerves)

Key Ingredients

  • Brahmi (Bacopa monnieri) – Primary nootropic; enhances memory and neuronal function • Vacha (Acorus calamus) – Improves speech, clears mental fog, and enhances channel clarity • Shankhapushpi (Convolvulus pluricaulis) – Calms the mind and reduces stress • Kushtha (Saussurea lappa) – Supports Vata balance and nervous system stability • Trikatu (Ginger, Black Pepper, Long Pepper) – Enhances digestion and bioavailability • Go Ghrita (Cow Ghee) – Nourishes brain tissues (Majja Dhatu) and carries herbs deep into the system

Important Note

  • Brahmi Ghritam is intended for internal use only. It is usually administered in small quantities, often with warm milk or warm water, depending on the condition and physician’s advice.
  • Because it is a potent neuro-nourishing formulation, dosage and duration should ideally be guided by an Ayurvedic practitioner.

Dadimadi Ghritam

Dadimadi Ghritam is a classical Ayurvedic formulation belonging to the category of Ghrita (medicated ghee). It is named after its primary ingredient Dadima (Pomegranate), which is renowned for its role in digestive strengthening and blood nourishment. This formulation is widely used as a Deepana (digestive stimulant) and Raktaprasadana (blood-enriching) medicine. What makes it unique is its ability to simultaneously correct digestive weakness (Agni Mandya) and improve blood quality, making it highly useful in Vata–Pitta disorders involving digestion and circulation. Dadimadi Ghritam is especially valued in conditions where poor digestion leads to anemia, weakness, and systemic imbalance.

Guggulu Tiktaka Ghritam

Guggulu Tiktaka Ghritam is a classical Ayurvedic formulation belonging to the category of Ghrita (medicated ghee). It combines the powerful resin Guggulu (Commiphora mukul) with a group of Tikta (bitter) herbs, creating a formulation designed for deep tissue detoxification and chronic inflammatory disorders. This medicine is widely used as a Kleda-hara (removes pathological moisture/toxins) and Vata-hara (pacifies Vata) formulation. It is especially valued for its ability to penetrate into deeper tissues such as Asthi (bone) and Majja (bone marrow), making it highly effective in chronic, stubborn diseases involving joints, skin, and metabolism.
